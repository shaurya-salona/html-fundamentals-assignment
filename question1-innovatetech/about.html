Our Journey

From a small startup to a leading technology company, here's how we've grown and evolved.

  1. 2018

    Company Founded

    InnovateTech Solutions was founded by a group of passionate developers who believed in the power of technology to transform businesses. Started with just 5 team members and a vision to innovate.

  2. 2019

    First Major Client

    Secured our first enterprise client, a Fortune 500 company looking to modernize their legacy systems. This project established our reputation for delivering complex solutions on time and within budget.

  3. 2020

    Remote-First Approach

    Pivoted to a fully remote work model during the global pandemic, proving that great technology solutions can be delivered from anywhere. Expanded our team to 25 members across 8 countries.

  4. 2021

    Cloud Specialization

    Launched our cloud migration and optimization services, helping businesses reduce costs by 40% while improving performance and scalability. Became a certified partner with major cloud providers.

  5. 2022

    AI Integration Services

    Introduced AI and machine learning solutions to help businesses automate processes and gain insights from their data. Completed over 100 AI implementation projects.

  6. 2023

    Global Expansion

    Opened offices in Europe and Asia, expanding our global reach. Reached 500+ completed projects and 200+ satisfied clients worldwide.

  7. 2024

    Innovation Lab Launch

    Launched our Innovation Lab to research emerging technologies and develop cutting-edge solutions. Focus on sustainability and green technology initiatives.
